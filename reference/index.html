The quiet cost

How many prayers slipped away
while the screen kept pulling?

The adhān calls, and "just one more minute" becomes a missed window. It isn't a lack of faith — it's a notification, a feed, a thumb that keeps moving. The duty is real, and so is the distraction sitting in your pocket. Iqama simply puts the distraction down, until you've stood for the prayer.

How it works

Three steps. Five times a day.

  1. The window opens

    Prayer times are calculated on-device from your location. When a salah enters, the apps you chose lock.

  2. “Did you pray?”

    A calm shield meets you instead of the feed. Pray with presence — there's a free five-minute escape hatch if you truly need it.

  3. “I prayed” — and the streak grows

    Confirm, and your apps unlock. Each day you keep the five builds a quiet streak — a record kept only for you.
